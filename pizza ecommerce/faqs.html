Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?
A: To place an order, simply go to our menu page and select the items you'd like to order. Then, proceed to checkout and follow the instructions to complete your order.
Q: Can I customize my pizza?
A: Yes, you can customize your pizza by selecting your preferred toppings and crust type during the ordering process.
Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Delivery times may vary depending on your location and current demand. Generally, we strive to deliver orders within 30-45 minutes.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept cash on delivery as well as online payments via credit/debit cards and digital wallets.
